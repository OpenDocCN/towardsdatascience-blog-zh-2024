Model Reviews

A model review can be the culmination of a project, but it can also be a nice penultimate step before a report or model is completed and productionalized. Let me first explain what I mean by a model review, since this is not necessarily part of everyone‚Äôs process.

A model review is a presentation, complete with visuals and documentation, that explains the project (usually model training) you have completed from start to finish.
